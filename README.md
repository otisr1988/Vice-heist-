# 🌴 Vice Heist Slots 🌴

Welcome to **Vice Heist**, a retro synthwave 1980s Miami Vice-themed mobile slot machine game! This game is built to run perfectly on all devices, especially on **iPhone Safari**, with large finger-friendly buttons, solid neon colors, and a full heist-bankroll system.

---

## 🕶️ Game Features

*   **🎰 Neon Wilds**: When a Neon Wild lands on the middle columns, it shoots lasers and expands to fill the entire reel!
*   **🗝️ Heist Keys (Scatters)**: Landing 3 or more Heist Keys triggers the **Free Spins Heist Mode** where wild reels become permanent!
*   **📈 Paylines**: 10 distinct retro-styled win lines with diagnostic visualizations.
*   **💰 Bankroll & Stats**: Live trackers for your heist balance, biggest win, and session heist history.

---

## 🕹️ How to Set It Up (Step-by-Step)

Running this game on your own iPhone or sharing it with friends is super easy and 100% free using GitHub.

### Step 1: Upload the Files
Look in your repository. Make sure you have:
1.  `index.html` (This is the complete slot machine file).
2.  `README.md` (This helpful file!).

### Step 2: Turn on GitHub Pages (This makes it a playable website!)
1.  Go to your GitHub repository online: **github.com/your-username/Vice-heist**.
2.  Click on the **Settings** gear icon at the top of the page.
3.  On the left-side menu, scroll down and click on **Pages**.
4.  Under **Build and deployment**, look for **Branch**.
5.  Change it from `None` to **`main`** (or `master`), and keep folders as `/ (root)`.
6.  Click the **Save** button!

### Step 3: Play on Your Phone!
*   Wait about 30 to 60 seconds for GitHub to build the page.
*   Refresh that same Pages settings tab. You will see a green banner with a link like:
    `https://your-username.github.io/Vice-heist/`
*   Open that link on your **iPhone Safari** browser.
*   **Add to Home Screen**: In Safari, tap the Share button (the square with the arrow pointing up) and select **"Add to Home Screen"** to make it look and feel like a real downloaded app!
